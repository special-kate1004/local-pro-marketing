FAQ

How does the Voolt Pro free trial work?

Voolt runs Google and Facebook ad campaigns to deliver 5 fully verified, real leads. You're in direct contact with these leads, have full control over closing deals, and the trial is completely free with no payments or commitments. After the trial, you can add funds to your Voolt balance to continue generating leads.

How long does the free trial last?

The free trial is based on the number of leads (5 verified leads) rather than time. After the initial 5 leads, you can add budget to your Voolt balance to continue receiving more jobs and calls.

How does Voolt Marketing help me grow my business?

Voolt Marketing uses automated Google and Facebook ads to put your business in front of local customers, ensuring maximum visibility without the stress of managing ads. The result: More ready-to-book leads, more revenue, and less effort.

What makes Voolt Marketing different from traditional ads?

Traditional advertising is expensive, time-consuming, and requires manual targeting, ad copy, design, budgeting, and optimization. Voolt's AI-driven platform automates and continuously optimizes ads to reach local customers ready to book. Plus, you get real-time lead notifications, instant response capabilities, and affordability compared to hiring a marketing expert.

How many leads will I receive with my budget?

Lead volume depends on budget, industry, and local competition. Here's what you can typically expect:

  • Starter Plan ($250)*: Approx. 10 leads
  • Growth Plan ($500)*: Approx. 10 to 16 leads
  • Pro Plan ($750)*: More than 16 leads

*Pricing may vary based on industry and location.

How will I receive my new leads?

When a potential customer clicks your ad and submits a form, their information is instantly sent to your Voolt dashboard, along with email and SMS notifications. If they call instead, the call is recorded and saved in your dashboard for easy access. Track and manage all leads in one place to avoid missing opportunities.

How much do I need to invest?

Voolt Marketing is flexible and budget-friendly. Start with the Starter Plan for just $175* and receive up to 10 leads. Upgrade for more leads:

  • Growth Plan ($500)*: 10 to 16 leads
  • Pro Plan ($750)*: 16+ leads

There are no monthly fees, just results.

*Pricing may vary based on industry and location.
